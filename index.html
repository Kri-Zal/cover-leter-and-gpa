How to Register a .com.np Domain in Nepal

A step-by-step guide to the process managed by Mercantile Communications Pvt. Ltd.

Step 1 — Check Availability

Visit register.com.np and search your desired domain. The name must closely match your legal name (personal) or registered company name (organization). Unrelated names are not approved.

Step 2 — Create Account

Register on register.com.np with your email and verify it. Use the same email in your cover letter. After verifying, proceed to the domain registration form.

Step 3 — Prepare Documents

Required documents:

  • Personal: Scanned citizenship, passport, or voter card
  • Organization: Company reg. certificate, PAN/VAT, stamped letterhead
  • Both: This cover letter + configured nameservers

Step 4 — Submit & Wait

Upload your cover letter JPEG and documents through the portal. Ensure all details match exactly. Approval typically takes 1–3 working days after manual review.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a .com.np domain really free? +
Yes. Nepal's .np ccTLD domains including .com.np, .org.np, and .edu.np are provided free of charge by Mercantile Communications Pvt. Ltd. under the Nepal Telecommunications Authority. There are no registration or renewal fees.
Why is a cover letter required? +
Mercantile Communications uses a manual review process. The cover letter confirms your identity, shows that the domain name matches your name or organization, and documents your nameserver details. Applications without a properly formatted cover letter are frequently delayed or rejected.
Can I choose any domain name I want? +
No. For personal domains the name must closely reflect your legal name as shown on your identity document. For organizations it must align with your registered company name. Arbitrary names, trademarks you don't own, or names unrelated to your identity are rejected.
How long does domain approval take? +
Typically 1 to 3 working days. During high-volume periods it can take longer. A correct, complete cover letter and clear scanned documents significantly reduce processing time.
How accurate is the NEB GPA calculator? +
It uses the official NEB grading system and weighted credit-hour formula. Science subjects use a 75% theory / 25% practical split (Computer Science uses 50/50). Management subjects are 100% theory. The formula is GPA = Σ(Credit Hours × Grade Point) ÷ Total Credit Hours.
What does NG mean in NEB grading? +
NG (Non-Graded) means the student scored below 30% and did not meet the minimum passing threshold. It contributes 0.0 grade points to the GPA calculation. A re-examination (back exam) is required to clear the subject.
Why download the cover letter as JPEG and not PDF? +
Mercantile Communications' submission portal accepts image formats for cover letter uploads. A high-quality JPEG under 200KB is the most reliable format that renders clearly during manual review and fits within the portal's upload limits.
