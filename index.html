How to Register a .com.np Domain

Step-by-step process managed by Mercantile Communications Pvt. Ltd., Durbar Marg, Kathmandu

1. Check Availability

Go to register.com.np and search your desired domain. The name must closely match your legal name (personal) or your registered organization name. Arbitrary names are rejected.

2. Create an Account

Sign up on register.com.np with your email and password. Confirm your email via the link Mercantile sends. Use the same email address in your cover letter.

3. Prepare Documents

What you need varies by applicant:

  • Personal: Scanned citizenship, passport, voter card, or driving license
  • Organization: Company registration certificate, PAN/VAT, cover letter on letterhead with official stamp
  • Both: This cover letter + nameserver details from your host

4. Submit Application

Upload your cover letter (JPEG) and supporting documents through the register.com.np portal. Ensure all names match exactly across your letter and identity documents. Approval takes 1–3 working days.

Frequently Asked Questions

.com.np domains — are they really free? +
Yes. Nepal's .np ccTLD (including .com.np, .org.np, .edu.np) is provided free of charge by Mercantile Communications Pvt. Ltd. under the Nepal Telecommunications Authority. There are no registration or renewal fees.
Can I register any domain name I want? +
No. For personal domains, the name must closely match your legal name as shown on your citizenship or passport. For organizations, it must align with your registered company name. Arbitrary names, trademarks you don't own, and brand-unrelated names are rejected by Mercantile during manual review.
Why is a cover letter required? +
Mercantile Communications uses manual verification. The cover letter formally documents your identity, confirms the domain name matches your legal identity, and records the technical nameserver details. Applications without a properly formatted letter are commonly delayed or rejected — it is a mandatory requirement, not optional.
What nameservers should I enter? +
Your nameservers are provided by your hosting company. You'll find them in your hosting dashboard or the welcome email your host sent you. Common formats are ns1.hostname.com and ns2.hostname.com. Set up your hosting account before submitting so the nameservers are already active.
Why download as JPEG and not PDF? +
Mercantile's submission portal accepts image uploads for cover letters. A JPEG under 200KB is the most reliably accepted format for submission. The generator automatically compresses the image to stay within 200KB while keeping text sharp and readable for manual review.
How accurate is the NEB GPA calculator? +
The calculator uses the official NEB grading system: correct credit hours per subject, theory/practical split (75/25 for Science, 50/50 for Computer Science, 100% theory for most Management subjects), and the official grade point scale (A+ = 4.0 down to D = 1.6, NG = 0.0). The GPA formula is Σ(Credit Hour × Grade Point) ÷ Total Credit Hours.
What does NG mean and how does it affect GPA? +
NG (Non-Graded) means the student scored below 30% in a subject and did not earn a passing grade. It carries a grade point of 0.0. Getting NG in any single subject results in an overall NG status for the final result — the GPA calculation will still show a number, but you must retake the subject to achieve a passing result.
